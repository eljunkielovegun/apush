Study Tips

Use timelines as reference points

The visual timelines help you see connections between events and understand cause-and-effect relationships.

Focus on key themes across periods

Rather than memorizing isolated facts, look for continuing themes like expansion, reform movements, civil rights struggles, and economic development.

Understand historical context

When studying specific events, consider what else was happening at that time.

Practice with APUSH-style questions

APUSH tests often include stimulus-based multiple choice and short answer questions.
